Partnering in Zululand’s economic future.

eNani Digital Tools is based in Ncotshane Township, Pongola, and works with clients across Zululand and KwaZulu-Natal. We support local economic development by providing the digital infrastructure that enterprises, cooperatives, and development programmes need to grow.

Our work complements:

  • The Zululand District Growth and Development Plan.
  • The province’s agriculture and rural development priorities.
  • National efforts to strengthen township and rural economies.

We focus on listening to local context, co-designing practical digital solutions, building capacity through training, and providing long-term support.
