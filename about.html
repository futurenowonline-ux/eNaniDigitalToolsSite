Partnering in Zululand’s economic future.

eNani Digital Tools is based in Ncotshane Township, Pongola, and serves clients across Zululand and KwaZulu-Natal. We support local economic development by providing the digital infrastructure that enterprises, cooperatives, and development programmes need to grow, compete, and thrive.

Our work complements the Zululand District Growth and Development Plan, the KwaZulu-Natal Department of Agriculture and Rural Development priorities, and national efforts to strengthen township and rural economies. We focus on making digital transformation accessible, practical, and affordable—not expensive or complex.
